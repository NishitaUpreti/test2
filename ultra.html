Tata Power Solar, a recognized leader in the solar energy sector in India, has consistently demonstrated its ability to design and deliver solar solutions efficiently, meeting the diverse needs of industries across the country. The company has earned a reputation for its proven capabilities in solar technology, with an unblemished record of delivering projects on time and within budget. This success has made Tata Power Solar the preferred choice for companies looking to meet their Renewable Purchase Obligation (RPO) while transitioning to clean energy solutions.

Project Overview

  • Project Name: 2.5 MW Solar Power Project for UltraTech Cement
  • Capacity: 2.5 MW (25 solar stations of 100 kWp each)
  • Locations: 6 facilities across 5 states (Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Rajasthan)
  • Project Timeline: 5 months
  • Objective: To fulfill UltraTech Cement's RPO requirements by generating 0.5% of its total energy from renewable sources.
  • Challenges:
    • Accessing remote locations with limited transport options
    • Customizing design elements for diverse site conditions
    • Managing simultaneous execution across multiple sites

Impact and Results

  • Total Capacity: The combined capacity of the 25 solar power plants is 2.5 MW. This significant contribution helps UltraTech Cement meet its RPO obligation, ensuring that 0.5% of its total energy consumption comes from renewable sources.
  • Cost Savings: Beyond fulfilling the RPO, the solar power plants have provided energy cost savings for UltraTech Cement. The solar installations not only serve as a cleaner energy source but also reduce the company's reliance on conventional power, lowering long-term energy costs.
  • Environmental Impact: By adopting solar energy, UltraTech Cement has significantly reduced its carbon footprint, supporting the nation's transition to clean energy and contributing to the broader goal of sustainability.
