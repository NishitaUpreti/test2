Tata Power Solar has consistently demonstrated expertise in delivering sustainable energy solutions to some of the most remote and challenging regions in India. Known for their innovative approach and strict adherence to timelines and budgets, Tata Power Solar has become a trusted partner for microgrid projects in areas where traditional grid connectivity is not feasible. One such success story is the microgrid project in Indrapur, a village in the Sundarbans. Through a custom-designed 110 kW solar plant, Tata Power Solar has brought much-needed electricity to this vibrant but isolated community, enabling significant improvements in quality of life and economic growth. This report highlights the project's key elements, challenges, and impacts, showcasing Tata Power Solar's commitment to empowering remote communities with clean, sustainable energy solutions.

Project Overview

Tata Power Solar has successfully executed a microgrid project in the remote village of Indrapur, Sundarbans, providing an accessible and sustainable source of energy. Located 81 kilometers from Howrah, the village can only be reached by a 90-minute ferry ride. In March 2011, Tata Power Solar commissioned a custom-designed, 110 kW solar plant to meet the community's needs, enabling over 2,000 families to access electricity for the first time.

Challenges and Solutions

  1. Inaccessible Location:
    • Challenge: The site could only be accessed by ferry, making transport of materials challenging.
    • Solution: Tata Power Solar coordinated shipments and work schedules to ensure timely delivery despite transportation limitations.
  2. Limited Sunlight:
    • Challenge: The region has intermittent sunlight, impacting continuous solar energy access.
    • Solution: A unique two-day autonomy system was implemented. The battery bank was configured to discharge 25-30% daily, maintaining a reserve of 70-80% to be used on cloudy days.

Impact

  • Access to Electricity: The solar power plant now supports a community of 10,000, giving them reliable access to electricity and clean water.
  • Economic and Social Empowerment: The project has improved literacy rates and economic opportunities. Families enjoy extended hours of activity, including night bazaars and improved lighting for students to study in the evening. The island's economy has flourished, benefiting from extended market hours.

This project in Sundarbans is an exemplary model of Tata Power Solar's commitment to sustainable and accessible energy for remote communities, bringing lasting change to the people of Indrapur.
